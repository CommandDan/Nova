enchantmentValue

open override val enchantmentValue: Int

The enchantment value of this item. Items with a higher enchantment value have a higher chance of getting more secondary enchantments when enchanted in the enchanting table.

As an example, these are the vanilla enchantment values depending on the material:

  • Wood: 15

  • Stone: 5

  • Iron: 14

  • Diamond: 10

  • Gold: 22

  • Netherite: 15
